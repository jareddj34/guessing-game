Which instrument is usually the heaviest?

The Piano is typically the heaviest. Smaller pianos can weigh up to a few hundred pounds, while larger grand pianos can weigh over 1000 pounds!

What family of instruments does the clarinet belong to?

The clarinet is a woodwind instrument. Woodwind instruments are classified by blowing air through a mouthpiece, and changing the pitch by opening and closing holes on the instrument.

Which instrument is commonly associated with jazz music?

The saxophone is commonly associated with jazz music. It was invented by Adolphe Sax in 1846. Some of the most famous sax players include: Charlie Parker, Ornette Coleman, John Coltrane, and much more.

Where did the guitar most likely originate from?
